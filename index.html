Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Overview

Prayer Lock ("we", "our", or "the app") is a spiritual habit app that helps you build a daily prayer practice. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data stored on your device

All personal data is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate servers that store your personal information. This includes:

  • Your prayer sessions and generated prayers
  • Mood and spiritual well-being ratings
  • Your prayer streak and statistics
  • Profile information you enter during onboarding (age group, denomination, goals, etc.)
  • App settings and preferences

If you delete the app, all of this data is permanently deleted from your device.

AI prayer generation

When you start a prayer session, the app sends the following information to our secure proxy server to generate a personalised prayer:

  • Your current mood rating (a number from 1–6)
  • Your current relationship-with-God rating (a number from 1–6)
  • Anonymised profile context (age group, denomination, goals — no name or contact details)

This request is routed through our Cloudflare Workers proxy, which forwards it to DeepSeek AI to generate the prayer text. Neither our proxy nor DeepSeek stores this data after the request is complete.

DeepSeek's privacy policy: deepseek.com/privacy

Third-party services

  • Cloudflare Workers — routes AI requests. Processes data in transit only. Privacy policy
  • DeepSeek AI — generates prayer text. Privacy policy
  • Sentry — crash reporting. If the app crashes, an anonymous error report (no personal data) may be sent. Privacy policy

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not require account registration

Push notifications

The app may send local push notifications to remind you to pray. These are scheduled entirely on your device — no data leaves your device for this feature. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings.

Children's privacy

Prayer Lock is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
